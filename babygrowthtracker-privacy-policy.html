Privacy Policy

BabyGrowthTracker – Last updated: October 18, 2025

BabyGrowthTracker (“we”, “our”, “us”) is a mobile application created to help parents track their baby’s growth in a simple and secure way. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your data.

Important: BabyGrowthTracker stores all user data locally on your device. No data is transmitted to or stored on external servers. We do not share or sell any information.

Information We Handle

The app allows you to input and manage baby-related information, such as:

  • Baby’s name or nickname
  • Date of birth and gender
  • Weight, height, and head circumference records
  • Optional profile photo

This data is stored only on your device using secure local storage (AsyncStorage). BabyGrowthTracker does not collect, upload, or send this information to any external database or third party.

Permissions Used

  • Camera / Photos: Used only if you choose to take or select a baby profile photo. The image remains on your device.
  • Storage: Used to save your baby’s data locally on your device.
  • Notifications (optional): Used for local reminders (e.g., measurement time). No remote push notifications are sent.

All permissions are optional and you can manage them at any time in your device settings.

Data Usage and Sharing

We use your locally stored information only to provide the app’s features, such as displaying charts or reminders. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any external party or analytics provider.

Data Security

Because all information is stored locally on your device, your data security depends primarily on your device’s security settings. We recommend keeping your device locked with a passcode or biometrics and installing operating system updates regularly.

Children’s Privacy

BabyGrowthTracker is designed for use by parents or guardians. It is not directly targeted at children, and the app does not collect data from children without parental input. All entered data is managed solely by the parent user.

Your Rights and Controls

  • You can view, edit, or delete all entered information directly within the app.
  • You can reset or delete all stored data by uninstalling the app or using the in-app reset option (if available).
  • You can revoke permissions (e.g., camera, storage) at any time through device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if we introduce new features or change how data is handled. Any significant updates will be reflected by changing the “Last updated” date above.
